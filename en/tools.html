MAILGUESSINT BETAprivate

What it does

  • – Mix of NAMINT and Holehe
  • – From a first name + last name, generates the most likely email address combinations
  • – Tests their existence across 120+ services via Holehe (no API key needed) with rate limit control
  • – Checks MX/DNS records for the domain
  • – Queries HaveIBeenPwned for data breaches (API key required)
  • – Enrichment via Epieos (API key required)
  • – Produces a consolidated report of results

Why I built it

During a real investigation involving an uncommon first and last name, this tool was designed to speed up the search and identify email addresses usable as pivot points.
Result: 2 cases tested, 2 emails "guessed".


SPORTINT BETAprivate

What it does

  • – Scrapes public activities from sports platforms (Komoot, Strava coming soon)
  • – Extracts: activity type, date, GPS coordinates (start/end), distance, duration
  • – Export CSV, JSON, GPX or ZIP (CSV + GPX + GeoJSON + photos)
  • – Dedicated ZeroNeurone export: enriched GeoJSON with all properties (type, date, distance, duration…) natively supported
  • – Allows retracing a target's movements from their public profile

Why I built it

Built during a real investigation, the work relied on a large volume of public sports activities. Exploiting this data and the geographic points was decisive for the rest of the investigation, making it possible to identify habits, places of residence and other pivot points.
